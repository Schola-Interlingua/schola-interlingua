Privacy Policy

Schola Interlingua for web, Android, iOS, and macOS.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Schola Interlingua collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the website or the app on Android, iOS, or macOS.

2. Information we may collect

  • Account information: if you sign in, we may process your e-mail address and authentication-related metadata through Supabase.
  • Learning data: lesson completion, reading completion, spaced repetition review state, vocabulary review results, and related progress history.
  • App settings: selected language, dark mode preference, and local device preferences needed for the app to work well.
  • Technical and operational data: service logs, device or browser information, and request metadata processed by hosting or backend infrastructure for security, reliability, and debugging.
  • Chat or support data: if you use Chatina or related chat/support features, the content of those interactions may be processed by the relevant provider.

3. How we use information

  • To authenticate users and maintain accounts.
  • To save and synchronize study progress across devices.
  • To provide review systems such as spaced repetition and quiz history.
  • To operate, secure, improve, and troubleshoot the service.
  • To communicate about service issues or respond to support requests.

4. Legal bases

Depending on where you are located, we may process information based on performance of a contract, your consent, our legitimate interests in operating the service, or compliance with legal obligations.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us run Schola Interlingua, such as:

  • Supabase for authentication and progress storage.
  • Hosting and delivery providers for serving the website and app assets.
  • Chat or support providers if you interact with Chatina or embedded support features.

6. Data retention

We retain account and progress data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain continuity across devices, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

7. Local storage and device storage

Some settings and progress data may be stored locally on your device or browser, especially when you use the app without signing in.

8. Children’s privacy

Schola Interlingua is an educational platform, but it is not specifically directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law to provide personal data without parental authorization. If you believe personal data from a child has been provided improperly, please contact us.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information, and to request portability where applicable.

11. International processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our service providers operate.

12. External services

Links to other services, repositories, community groups, or third-party chat widgets may direct you to platforms that have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services outside Schola Interlingua.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will publish the updated version at this URL.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the project through the official repository or the community group at t.me/scholainterlingua.
