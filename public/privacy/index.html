Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Schola Interlingua is a language-learning platform for studying Interlingua. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as your email address when you sign in.
  • Learning progress and app preferences needed to provide the service.
  • Technical information such as device, browser, and diagnostic data.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • To sync your learning progress across devices.
  • To improve reliability, security, and product quality.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us run authentication, hosting, storage, and support infrastructure.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

You can request account deletion from the app or by following the instructions on our account deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the project repository or the official Schola Interlingua channels.
