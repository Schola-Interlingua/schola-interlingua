Delete Account

Last updated: June 24, 2026

You can delete your Schola Interlingua account directly from the app if you are signed in.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Schola Interlingua and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select the option to delete your account.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

What Happens Next

  • Your authentication session will be closed.
  • Your local app data will be cleared from the current device.
  • Associated account data will be scheduled for removal from our systems.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app and need help deleting your account, contact us through the official Schola Interlingua support channels or project repository.
